Modern Architecture

Modern architecture focuses on clean lines, open spaces, and functional design. It uses materials such as glass, steel, and concrete to create structures that are both efficient and visually strong. Natural light, geometry, and minimal decoration are key characteristics of this style.

Sustainable buildings use energy-efficient materials and eco-friendly construction techniques to reduce environmental impact.

Architecture Planning Objective

Architectural planning balances aesthetics, safety, and usability. A good design considers space utilization, ventilation, lighting, structure strength, and user comfort. Floor plans, room relationships, service areas, and circulation paths must be planned before construction. Proper architectural design improves functionality while maintaining visual harmony across the entire structure.

Green architecture integrates landscape, sunlight direction, and energy systems into the building layout.
